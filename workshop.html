QT
The Qt Frameworks Workshop, organized by YOTA at Bahria University, introduced students to developing GUI-based applications using the Qt framework. The hands-on session covered setting up Qt Creator, creating intuitive user interfaces with C++, and real-world applications. Participants engaged in coding exercises and problem-solving, guided by experienced mentors. The workshop inspired students to explore GUI development and highlighted YOTA’s commitment to advancing technical skills and innovation among young developers.

SFML
The SFML Frameworks Workshop, organized by YOTA at Bahria University, introduced students to developing multimedia-rich applications using the SFML (Simple and Fast Multimedia Library) framework. The hands-on session covered setting up SFML with C++, creating engaging 2D graphics, and building real-time interactive applications. Participants engaged in coding exercises and problem-solving activities, guided by experienced mentors. The workshop inspired students to explore multimedia application development and highlighted YOTA’s commitment to advancing technical skills and innovation among young developers.

Code Quest
The Code Quest Preparation Session, organized by YOTA at Bahria University, was an exclusive initiative designed to empower female students in competitive programming. This session focused on enhancing problem-solving skills, mastering algorithms, and understanding coding techniques essential for coding competitions. Guided by experienced mentors, participants tackled a series of hands-on challenges and real-world problems to boost their confidence and competence. The session highlighted YOTA’s commitment to creating an inclusive environment for women to excel in the world of competitive programming.

Teaching Initiative
The Teaching Initiative Session, organized by YOTA at Bahria University Islamabad Campus (BUIC), aimed to train university students in effectively teaching school-level students. This session focused on developing essential teaching techniques, communication skills, and creative methods to make learning engaging and impactful. Participants learned how to design interactive lesson plans, simplify complex concepts, and foster a supportive learning environment. Guided by experienced educators, students were equipped to inspire and mentor the next generation.
